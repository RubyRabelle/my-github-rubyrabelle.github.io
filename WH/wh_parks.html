Three parks in one neighborhood.

Whispering Hollow Parks

Living in Whispering Hollow you are always a quick walk to a variety of local park areas, playgrounds and green spaces.

Whispering Hollow Park One

Park One is adjacent to the pool and offers a covered Pavillion with charcoal grills and picnic tables. The pavillion is available for parties with a reservation from Goodwin Management, and is frequently the site of neighborhood parties and events like "South X WH". The play structure includes a slide and climbing structure, as well as sand-box play area.

Whispering Hollow Park Two

Park Two is primarily a playground designed for younger children. Located on Sugar Creek, it includes benches with shade, teeter-totters and a small play structure.

Buda Park @ Whispering Hollow

The City of Buda maintains a park overlooking Cold Water Hollow in Whispering Hollow. This park situated on several acres of greenbelt includes a covered pavillion, a covered play area, bathrooms, water fountains, city wi-fi and extra parking available on Old Black Colony Road.

Elm Grove Elementary Playground

Proximity to Elm Grove Elementary School also makes the Elm Grove Playground a popular destination for older children after school hours, Elm Grove has swings, covered picnic tables, multiple climbing structures with shade, a back stop and large field as well as multiple basketball hoops on a large court.
