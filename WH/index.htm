Activities

Whispering Hollow is a lovely neighborhood located just south of Austin, that hosts many fun activities for the family.

  • Relax by the pool
  • Take the kids to one of our three parks
  • SXWH - South By Whispering Hollow - live music & crawfish boil
  • Easter Egg hunts
  • Monthly Bunco
  • Santa visits every year
  • Mommies that Wine Club
