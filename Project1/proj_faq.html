Are they really strong enough to move furniture?
Our kids are young, strong and athletic. They have ample experience moving furniture like couches and tables up and down stairs and even hauling refrigerators.

As millenials, they are attached to their mobile devices. They can snap a quick picture of the item(s) you would like to sell, list it on multiple local selling platforms and even meet with the buyers for you.
