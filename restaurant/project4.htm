Aloha! Big Wave Dave first opened his Hawaiian BBQ Restaurant and Tiki Bar in 2015. Coming to Big Wave Dave's will make you feel like you are vacationing on the beach at a Hawaiian or Tahitian resort. We offer a generous mix of traditional Luau and mixed Polynesian Cuisine. Come sit at our Tiki Bar and enjoy a delicious Mai Tai or Scorpion while you wait for the evening's entertainment to begin.

We offer a casual beach dining atmosphere with live entertainment including fire dancers every Friday and Saturday night. Aloha!
